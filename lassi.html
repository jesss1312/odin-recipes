Mango Lassi


INGREDIENTS REQUIRED:

  • 1.5 cups mango-peeled,seeded,chopped and chilled

  • 1.5 cups plain youghurt

  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream

  • half cup cold milk

  • 2 tablespoons confextioners' sugar

  • half teaspoon ground cardamom


DIRECTIONS

  1. step 1: Combine mangos,youghurt,milk,cream,confextioners sugar,cardamom
  2. step 2: blend them in a grinder

YOUR LASSI IS READY TO BE SERVED
